About

I am a senior at Stuyvesant High School in New York City, and have been writing software for years. I am the President of Software Engineering on the Team 694 Robotics Team. (Our code is all open-source and available on GitHub.)

In 2016, Team 694 reached the world championship of FIRST Robotics and then went on to Einstein, the final competition between the victors of each of the eight divisions at the championship. This makes us the only active team in NYC to have gotten to Einstein. In the NYC Regional competition (a qualifier for the world championship), we won the Engineering Inspiration Award for, in part, the help we have offered to other teams, including in the China Robotics Challenge, and in the NYC Regional itself. We had a "Fix It crew" dedicated to aiding other teams, and although I was generally busy with my work for our own robot, I had a couple opportunities to help a couple other teams.

I have a passion for software development (and programming language design) which I have persued through personal projects.
